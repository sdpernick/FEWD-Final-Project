DC Slices

Neighborhood: 14th Street/U Street (Food Truck)

Metro Station: U Street/Cardozo

Cuisine: American (Pizza + Tots)

Price: $ (Under $20)

Good For: Everyone

Atmosphere: Causal

To determine what was best on my plate
We must reflect upon what I ate

Recollections of foie gras and spaghetti's squid ink
Enticing arrays of cheeses that stink

Charcuterie boards of Coppa and Speck
Molecular gastronomy: the pinnacle of high tech

Raw perfection is tuna tartare
Also ceviche, a Peruvian star

Sending taste buds on a spicy ride
With lamb tibs and injera on the side

Quail eggs and duck eggs and poached eggs oh my!
Globetrotting through Asia with a bowl of Pad Thai

A chocolate confection adorned with bon bons
And goat cheese enchiladas with deep water prawns

Although scrumptious as these foods may seem
That they top my list is merely a dream

The best bites of life need not be frilly
And hardcore foodies may find me silly

Truly American, simple and fried
From my fingers they cannot be pried

From dawn's early light to eve's nightfall
These delectable wonders are a diet's downfall

Glistening tubers smothered in Old Bay is my scene
For which DC food trucks reign supreme

With a light layer of grease running down my chin
Bursting in flavor so good it's a sin

These morsels of warmth and salty flavor
Never eaten slowly enough to savor

Fried until golden flawless they be
Loaded tater tots are perfection for me
